Italian Pasta

Ingredients:

200g pasta of your choice (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.)
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 cups tomato passata (or canned crushed tomatoes)
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste
Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

Cook the Pasta:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes).
3. Once cooked, drain the pasta and set aside. Reserve a small amount of pasta water.
Prepare the Sauce:
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté until softened and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
3. Pour in the tomato passata or crushed tomatoes. Stir in dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
4. Let the sauce simmer gently for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly and the flavors meld together.
5. If the sauce becomes too thick, you can add a splash of reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
Combine Pasta and Sauce:
1. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the sauce.
2. Toss the pasta gently in the sauce until it is well coated and heated through.
Serve:
1. Divide the pasta among serving plates or bowls.
2. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, if desired.
3. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Italian pasta!
Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite ingredients such as cooked vegetables, grilled chicken, or shrimp. Buon appetito!
