Paneer butter masala

Ingredients:

250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon oil
1 onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, pureed
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 cup heavy cream
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
3. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the mixture thickens and the oil separates from the sides.
4. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add the cubed paneer to the masala and gently stir to coat the paneer evenly with the sauce.
6. Pour in the heavy cream and mix until well combined. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy your creamy and flavorful Paneer Butter Masala!
